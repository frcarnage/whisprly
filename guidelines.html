Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 2025

Whisprly is a community built on respect and safety. Please follow these guidelines:

Be Respectful

Treat others kindly. No hate speech, harassment, or bullying.

No Illegal Content

Do not post content that violates laws or promotes illegal activity.

Keep it Safe

Avoid sharing personal or sensitive information of yourself or others.

Report Violations

If you see content that breaks these guidelines, please report it to us.

Consequences

Violations may result in warnings, suspensions, or bans.
