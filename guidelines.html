Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 2025

Whisprly is a platform for open, safe, and honest expression. To keep our community welcoming and respectful, we ask all users to follow these guidelines:

1. Be Respectful

Treat everyone with kindness. Personal attacks, hate speech, harassment, and bullying will not be tolerated — whether in posts, chats, or comments.

2. Respect Anonymity

Many users prefer to post anonymously. Do not attempt to reveal or guess someone's identity. Respect everyone's privacy — even if you disagree with them.

3. No Harmful or Illegal Content

Posting content that promotes violence, self-harm, abuse, illegal activity, or discrimination is strictly prohibited.

4. No Spam or Manipulation

  • Don’t use bots or scripts to post or like content
  • No mass messaging, repetitive posts, or irrelevant links
  • No self-promotion, advertising, or misleading content

5. Use Reporting Responsibly

If you see something that violates these guidelines, use the in-app Report feature. False reporting or misuse of this tool may result in warnings or bans.

6. Age Requirement

You must be at least 13 years old to use Whisprly. Content inappropriate for minors is not allowed.

7. Violations and Enforcement

Breaking these rules may lead to content removal, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans. You will always be informed of the reason if action is taken against your account.

8. Appeals

If you feel your content or account was removed unfairly, you can appeal directly through the app. All appeals are reviewed carefully.

Let’s Keep Whisprly Safe for All

Your actions shape this community. Let's create a space where everyone feels heard and safe — whether posting publicly or anonymously.

Need Help?

Email us at support@whisprly.net for any questions or concerns.
