Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 2025

Whisprly is a place for open, safe, and honest expression. To keep our community positive and respectful, all users must follow these guidelines:

1. Respect Others

Treat everyone with kindness and fairness. Harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, bullying, or discriminatory content will not be tolerated in any form — whether in posts, chats, or comments.

2. Respect Anonymity & Privacy

Many Whisprly users post anonymously. Do not attempt to reveal, guess, or share someone’s personal identity. Protect your own privacy and respect the privacy of others.

3. No Harmful or Illegal Content

Do not post or promote content that includes:

  • Violence, threats, or incitement to harm
  • Encouragement of self-harm or suicide
  • Child exploitation or sexually explicit material
  • Illegal activity, scams, or abusive behavior

4. No Spam, Advertising, or Manipulation

  • No mass messaging, flooding, or irrelevant posting
  • No bots, scripts, or automation to manipulate engagement
  • No self-promotion, advertising, or misleading content without permission

5. Use Reporting Tools Responsibly

If you see content that breaks these rules, use the in-app Report feature. Do not misuse reporting to target or silence others unfairly — false reports may lead to warnings or account action.

6. Age Requirement

You must be at least 13 years old to use Whisprly. Content inappropriate for minors is strictly prohibited.

7. Enforcement

Breaking these rules may result in content removal, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans. You will always be informed of the reason if action is taken against your account.

8. Appeals

If you believe your content or account was removed unfairly, you can submit an appeal directly in the app. Appeals are reviewed within 5–7 business days.

Let’s Keep Whisprly Safe

Every action shapes the community. Let’s work together to create a safe, respectful space where everyone can express themselves openly — whether posting publicly or anonymously.

Need Help?

Email our support team at support@whisprly.net with any questions or concerns.
