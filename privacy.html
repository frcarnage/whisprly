Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

At Whisprly, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Optional information you choose to share in your profile or posts.
  • Technical data such as device type, operating system, and log data (for app performance and security).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain Whisprly’s core features.
  • Personalize your experience within the app.
  • Improve performance and troubleshoot issues.
  • Ensure community safety and prevent misuse.

3. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is stored securely through Firebase (Google) and media files are stored in Cloudinary. We apply industry-standard encryption and access controls to protect your information.

4. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Deactivate or permanently delete your account.
  • Contact us with questions about your data privacy.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies or third-party tracking technologies. Any app analytics are strictly limited to app performance and crash reporting.

6. Children’s Privacy

Whisprly is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we may have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified in-app or via email where possible.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@whisprly.net
